Hello, dear visitor(s)!

The website you are currently seeing is the result of a whole semester of hard work for the PluriTAL Master's "Programmation et projet encadré" class (Paris III Sorbonne Nouvelle - Paris Ouest Nanterre-La Défense - INALCO), given by Serge FLEURY and Jean-Michel DAUBE.
It unifies all the development stages and the results of the project "La vie multilingue d'un mot sur le web". This project is the making of a chain of semi-automatic text processing applied to multilingual data.
Wich means we made a software able to search throught the text of particular websites and it extracts a specific word or group of words, so we are able to present and contextually interpret the data in various languages.

As you can see, our website is also available in French, Spanish and it's visually-impaired accessible.

The multilingual life of the "cochlear implant".
